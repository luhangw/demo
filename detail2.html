  今天音响/网站设计    TODAY SOUND


  今天音响是一家基于设备销售、提供音视频和会议系统整体解决方案的公司。基于其智能、专业、现代等特质，我们打造了一个冷静、硬朗、简洁的页面风格。


  TODAY SOUND is an equipment selling based,audio and video and conference system solution provider with a feature ofintelligence, dependability, and modernization. Accordingly, we built a calm,firm and clear design style.







  整体风格  Holistic style


  遵循现代主义建筑大师路德维希·密斯·凡德罗（Ludwig Mies Van der Rohe）“Less is more”与“流动空间”等概念，我们合并并简化板块、将建筑感融入页面设计、强调视觉冲击。


  In the light of the Modernist master ofarchitecture, Ludwig Mies Van der Rohe, we integrated architectonic feelinginto page design with his significant conception of Minimalism and Free FlowingSpace, simplified and merged categories, emphasized visual impact.








  细节及色彩  Details and Colors


  以黑白灰为主色调，利用大幅banner、框线icon、圆角矩形与直角矩形等几何元素互相冲撞，营造出空间感和都会感，高饱和度的蓝色则让一切灵动起来。The colorlessness dominant hue, giantbanners, graphics icons,  rectangles, and rounded rectanglesarise stereoscopic sense which accompanied a sense of Metropolis,           meanwhile,high saturated blue polishes all of the layout.
